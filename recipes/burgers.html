Lentil Burgers

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • Vegan beef stock
  • Onion
  • Mushroom
  • Chilli
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Garlic
  • Tomato paste

Steps

  1. Wash the lentils and cook with stock until tender, then drain
  2. Chop the onion, chilli, garlic and fry. Add tomato paste at the end
  3. Combine all ingredients and mash together. Season
  4. Shape the mixture into burger sized patties
  5. Shallow fry in oil for 5 mins on each side
  6. Serve in a bun with desired condiments and toppings
