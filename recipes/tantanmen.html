Tantanmen Ramen

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Sesame paste
  • Spicy bean paste
  • Miso paste
  • Mushroom stock
  • Soy milk
  • Vegan mince

Steps

  1. Fry the vegan mince with the miso paste and spicy broadbean paste
  2. In the ramen bowl, add sesame paste, stock and soy milk
  3. Cook the ramen noodles
  4. Top the ramen bowl off with boiling water and mix the soup base together
  5. Add the cooked noodles to the soup base
  6. Add the fried vegan mince on top
  7. Add any additional toppings you'd like, such as scallions, tofu, beansprouts or sweetcorn
