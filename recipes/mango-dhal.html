Mango Dhal

Ingredients

  • 1 large mango
  • 4 cups of red lentils
  • 1 tablespoon turmeric
  • 1 tablespoon mustard seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Mushroom stock

Steps

  1. Soak the lentils in water while preapring the rest of the ingredients
  2. Cut the mango into 2cm cubes
  3. Fry the spices in oil over medium heat until fragrant, then fry the mango
  4. Add the washed lentils and mushroom stock. Top up with water until the lentils are covered
  5. Simmer for one hour or until the lentils are the desired consistency
  6. Serve with rice or flatbread
